Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

At iDevelopment LLC, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our applications.

Information We Collect

Our applications are designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • CoolClean: This app runs entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal files, folder contents, or system information on our servers.
  • Purchase Information: When you make a purchase through the Mac App Store, Apple handles all payment processing. We receive confirmation of your purchase but do not have access to your payment details.

Data Storage

All data processed by our applications remains on your device. We do not:

  • Upload your files to any server
  • Track your file system activity
  • Collect analytics about your file contents
  • Share any information with third parties

App Sandboxing

Our applications are sandboxed according to Apple's guidelines. This means they can only access files and folders you explicitly grant permission to access. Your data never leaves your Mac.

Third-Party Services

Our applications do not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external service used is Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases, which is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

Our applications are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
